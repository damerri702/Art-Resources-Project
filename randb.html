R&B: My Favorite Artists

UMI

Tierra "Umi" Wilson is an American singer and songwriter from Seattle, Washington. I first discovered her back in 2019, and my favorite songs of hers are "High School", "Lullaby", and "Love Affair".

SiR

Sir Darryl Farris, known mononymously as Sir, is an American singer from Inglewood, California. I first discovered him back in 2018, and my favorite tracks of his are "D'Evils", "Satisfaction", and "Hair Down".

Xavier Omar

I first discovered Xavier Omar in 2018 through the song "Blind Man". Since then he has quickly become one of my favorite artists with songs like "How Do You Love Me" and "If This Is Love".

Jorja Smith

Jorja Smith is a singer-songwriter from the UK. I was first introduced to her in 2019 and since then my favorites songs of hers include "Something In The Way", "Teenage Fantasy", and "Be Honest"
